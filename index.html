Platform Market Share & Volume
PhonePe leads with 41.1% of all transactions and ₹2.7M in total volume. GPay follows at 35.4%. Together they create a duopoly controlling 76.5% of the UPI market.
Market Share
Monthly Transaction Volume Growth (2023–2024)
PhonePe consistently leads across all 24 months. Notable spikes appear in Oct 2023 and Jul 2024 — aligning with festival seasons. All platforms show gradual growth year-over-year.
Growth Trend
Peak Payment Hours
6 PM is the single busiest hour. Evening (5–8 PM) and lunch (12–1 PM) together drive over 40% of daily transaction volume.
Time Pattern
Spending by Category
Food & Dining tops at ₹1,468K. Bills & Recharge and Shopping closely follow — these three categories account for 60%+ of total spend.

City × Platform Transaction Heatmap
Mumbai × PhonePe is the hottest cell at ₹559K. Delhi and Bangalore follow. Metro cities drive the bulk of UPI volume — Tier 2 cities like Ahmedabad and Kolkata remain underpenetrated.

Observation

Analyst Recommendations

  • Targeted evening campaigns: Deploy cashback offers between 5–8 PM when transaction intent is highest across all platforms.
  • Food & Dining partnerships: PhonePe and GPay should deepen Swiggy/Zomato integrations — the category is already their #1 use case.
  • Tier 2 city push: Ahmedabad, Kolkata, and Chennai are underserved — localized marketing and merchant onboarding can unlock significant volume.
  • BHIM lesson for PMs: Government backing ≠ product adoption. Investing in UX and trust-building is non-negotiable in consumer fintech.
